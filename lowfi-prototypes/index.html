Low-Fidelity Prototypes for Recipe Page

Below are three different layout concepts for the recipe page, each including a **mobile** and **laptop** version.

Prototype 1 - Traditional Layout

Concept: A structured layout with a clear recipe title, ingredients list on the left, and step-by-step instructions on the right.

Prototype 2 - Image-Focused Layout

Concept: A visually rich layout with large images, bold section breaks, and step-by-step visuals.

Prototype 3 - Minimalist Layout

Concept: A clean, text-centered layout with collapsible sections for ingredients and instructions.
